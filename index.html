About Me

Ani Kochiashvili is Head of Portfolio Intelligence at Standard Metrics, where she builds the data architecture and analytical capabilities that underpin the firm's understanding of portfolio and fund performance. She designs scalable systems—integrating workflows, dashboards, and automated processes—that give Investment, IR, and Finance teams a unified, accurate, and real-time view of the business.

Before joining Standard Metrics, Ani was VP of Portfolio Analytics at Battery Ventures, where she built the core infrastructure supporting investment management and portfolio analytics. She unified data and workflows across Finance, Legal, Engineering, and the Investment teams, streamlining operations and enabling faster, more strategic decision-making.

Ani began her career at Deloitte, where she spent a decade advising Fortune 100 clients on strategy, operations, and financial workflows before moving into technology M&A, evaluating startup acquisition targets and collaborating on post-acquisition growth plans.

Across roles, she is known for bringing clarity to complexity—designing systems and analytics that scale, reduce friction, and elevate how organizations understand and use their data.
